Dark Adam

A console based text game made in C++ with an JRPG style, turn based combat and a focus around a story.

The game is set in a dystopian city, the game will follow the character Adam as he searches for the truth of what happened when he left 5 years ago. As the player and Adam explores what happened in the past, Adam must confront and justify the events to those he fought beside.

A card game program with 3 different card games in it, a simple 1 hand game of "Best Hand", a game of war vs. the computer and black jack with the computer as the dealer.
