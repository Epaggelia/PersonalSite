Movement

You can move between different rooms in different buildings to find things you can interact with for combat events, conversations or items you can pickup then equip or use.

Combat

Combat is turn based, based on the speed stats of each character you control and the enemies you are fighting. On each characters turn you select a target to attack and deal damage based on you attack stats reduced by the targets defense stats. The enemies attack in the same method.F

Chat

The game is strongly driven by its story so there are alot of conversation events which are back and forth comments with Adam and others. The chat itself scrolls down from the top of the screen with a key press needed when switching between each new line.
